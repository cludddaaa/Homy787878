Our future plan

At Homy Real Estate, we’re not just keeping pace with the evolving real estate landscape, we’re shaping its future. As we expand our footprint in affordable and market-rate housing, our strategic roadmap focuses on sustainability, technology, and community empowerment.

  • Develop 5,000+ new affordable units in partnership with HUD, LIHTC investors, and nonprofits.
  • Blockchain for transparent, fraud-resistant lease agreements.
  • Prioritize transit-oriented development (TOD) near bus/train lines to reduce commuting costs.
  • Modular construction to cut costs and speed up affordable housing delivery.
  • Launch a Housing Justice Fund to legally challenge discriminatory practices.
  • Target high-need areas like Atlanta, Detroit, and Houston.
